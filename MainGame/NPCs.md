# NPCs

## NPC types
The player will age up from the middle of grade school thorough middle school. Three years in Grade school and three in middle school. So all the NPCs will need to age up by six years over this time as well. Life changes will happen over this time period with people moving in and moving away, graduating and going off to college, getting married, having kids, moving the retirment home and passing on. 

### Senior
Older folks which may have kids and grandkids. Most are still independent, but some live in the retirement home. Their homes tend to be decorated in older styles with older items in them. Some of their yards are imacculate, but some show signs that they are struggling to keep it up. Their cars are either older or are a bit fancier than normal. Some are retired but some still work. More likely to be owners or administrators of the businesses or government positions than the younger folk.  

If they are affluent they are likely to have an RV or other recreational vehicles.

### Adult
Middle aged people who may have kids. The kids may be any age up to young adults. The ones with kids tend to have more homey or comforting decoration and style, and they are more likely to have a station wagon. The yards of the ones with kids tend to show some overflowing kid items. If they have very young children the upkeep of the yard may suffer. Childless couples have more modern and sleek styles or ornate styles. Single parents will have shabby yards with kid items in it and their interior is even more so. Childless single women may have several cats or dogs and have pet friendly decor. Chidless single men either have a very spartan interior or one overflowing with junk. 

Individuals with obsessions will have completed a noticable collection related to such by this time in life. Their hobbies may be well organized into a corner of their house or garage. 

If they are affuent their kids may go to the private school and their possesions will be more upscale. They may also have a boat or other vehicle for recreation. 

### Young adult
Younger adults just starting out. Some may have young children. Their styles can be more daring or experimental than the older folks. Their possesions are more modest but newer. Their vehicles are the same. They are more likely to live in an apartment or row home. More likely to have starter positions in their job.

### High schooler
Oldest kids, but not yet adults despite what some would like you to believe. They may have part time jobs or a side hustle like baby sitting. They live in some adults home usually, but sometimes you will run into one of these in a half way house having just got out of juvie or in an orphanage. They might have a vehicle of their own, but usually they just borrow an adult's vehicle. They have more money than the younger kids to persue their interest, but they are also worried about their impending adulthood and may be saving for college, a car, etc. 

The children of the more affluent adults may have a crazy computer setup or huge game selection. Other interests may be the same, like a complex music setup or expensive dirt bikes. 

### Middle schooler
Old enough to get in trouble and young enough not to know better. Still largely a dragged along with the adults, but they wish they had the freedom of the high schoolers. Enough skill in music, computers, etc. to be able to actually start to persue such interest in earnest. School is starting to ramp up requiring more time and effort, but there is still the ability to be mostly carefree at this age.  

### Grade schooler
Old enough to want to do some real things, but young enough that the adults still don't trust you at all. Many read well and are intersted in book and comics now. The desperation to grow up means they push their boundaries to see more intense movies, shows and games than maybe they really should see. Pushing boundaries on physical tasks can also lead to some craziness. Jumping bikes, skateboard tricks, dares to climb giant trees or cliffs etc. can lead to the most memorable hospital visits with a cool cast to be signed to boot.

While they start to have a bit of real independence with their own money and ability to spend it on their own that does not stop many of the stores from seeing these young kids as very suspicious. 

### Toddler
Young and carefree. Still totally dependent on their parents and will be drug along with them unless it is a special event and a babysitter is available. Getting into no end of trouble, but the chaos and sillyness is they primary entertainment they provide. 

### Nuns
Nuns can be adults of any age. They wear funny habits and are always single. They live in either the convent or in a nearby home often in pairs. Their style is always classical and a bit ornate despite also being spartan. They will always have some religeous iconography. If they live off campus then they might have a vehicle, but this will be a very modest vehicle.

They work in the church, convent, private school, retirement home, halfway house or orphanage. (Do we want to add specific areas for these latter two?) 

### Monks
Same a nuns, but male. Their habits are different but no less funny. There is a small cloister by the convent, but some live off campus as well.

They work in the same places the nuns do. 

### Priests
Religeous leader. Very similary to monks, but they wear a different uniform. The church has a head priest and an assistant. They also serve as adminstrator for the private shool, the orphanage, the retirement home, and the meditation center.
